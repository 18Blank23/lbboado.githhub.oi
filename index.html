Company Profile

LB BOADO CONSTRUCTION AND HAULING is the offshoot of San Lorenzo Sand and Gravel which was established in 1994 by Eduardo Oda Boado. With the continuous growth of the business, a new line of business sprouted - THE LB BOADO CONSTRUCTION AND HAULING

LB BOADO CONSTRUCTION AND HAULING is the brainchild of EDUARDO ODA BOADO and his spouse Lady Byrd Oris Boado, both hail from Binalonan, Pangasinan. The company is a single proprietorship engaged in the construction and hauling business.

LB BOADO CONSTRUCTION AND HAULING is a legitimate construction firm existing under the laws of the Philippines, Managed by a team dynamic and seasoned professionals who all come from the local construction industry. LB BOADO CONSTRUCTION AND HAULING upholds the standard of professionalism, integrity, moral values and continous innovation with industry best practices. Equipped with the necessary technical know-how and logistical resource, our valued clients are guaranteed of quality services and projects with integrety.
